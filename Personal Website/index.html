About

This is Minh Nguyen, a dynamic individual who proudly hails from the bustling city of Hanoi, Vietnam. Growing up in a vibrant cultural milieu, Minh was exposed to a rich tapestry of traditions, flavors, and experiences that shaped his worldview. Now, as a second-year student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Minh continues to embrace new horizons, both in his academic pursuits and his personal passions.

While Minh's studies in Financial Technology play a significant role in his life, there is much more to him than just numbers and algorithms. One of his greatest passions lies in the world of mixed martial arts, specifically Jiu Jitsu. From the moment he stepped onto the mat, Minh discovered an incredible sense of discipline, resilience, and personal growth through this ancient martial art. Jiu Jitsu has not only become a physical outlet for him but also a means of connecting with a diverse community of practitioners who share his zest for self-improvement.
